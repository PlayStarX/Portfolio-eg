My Top Favourite Movie Choices

First 9 by C.W.

What Women Want

A classic starring Mel Gibson:

A showgirls romeo raised in pleasurable ways. Educated through various male role models. A captivating story & comedy of someone who never settles for love.. or does he? ;)

40 days & 40 nights

Starring Josh Hartnett:

A guys previous relationship fails. Being hung up on his ex he enjoys the pleasures of partying around. After too much of the same thing he decides on a vow of abstinence. Challenged by his nature, will he succeed or call it a day? Give this comedy a go :)

Sin City

Starring Mickey Rourke, Josh Hartnett, Bruce Willis, Jessica Alba & some others:..

Defenders of Justice, the best on the market, above the rest. Vigilantes challenged by darkness trying to inhabit their peace. Good looking women & bad ass dudes in a sin filled city. Wanna see lifes heroes & villains? This actions worth a watch..

I Am Legend

Starring Will Smith

The soul survivor of an experiment gone wrong. Living day by day as he searches for any other survivors in his world. Challenged by infected humans out for flesh, & trying to maintain his sanity.. will he succeed in finding life? Perhaps he is the only survivor. What happens to his situation? A must watch for those with deep appreciation for alternate survival realities

Lord of the Rings

Starring Liv Tyler, Elijah Wood..

Our favorite fantasy, adventure, action release. Follow the series filled with battle, beauty, romance & love. Endless excitement for anyone who enjoys medievil tales.

Marley & Me

Starring Owen Wilson & Jennifer Anniston

A total heart wrencher about a dog.

Pirates of the Caribbean

Starring Johnny Depp, Kierra Knightley, Orlando Bloom..

The amazing adventures, journeys & fairytales of pirates

Supernatural

Starring Jensen Ackles, Jared Padalecki..

A series of 2 brothers who fight against everything evil to save humanity..

Passion of the Christ

Directed by Mel Gibson

A true story of the savior of the world. A man with the powers of GOD whos only crime was love, for all of mankind..
